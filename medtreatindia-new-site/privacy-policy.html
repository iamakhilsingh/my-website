Privacy Policy

How MedTreat India handles information shared through this website.

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Overview

MedTreat India provides medical travel guidance and coordination support. This website is designed to collect only the information a visitor chooses to share when requesting help.

Information You Share

When you submit a form or contact us, you may share your name, country, phone or WhatsApp contact, email address, treatment interest, travel preference, budget range, and message. You may also voluntarily share medical details later with a coordinator.

How We Use Information

We use the information to understand your request, respond to you, coordinate suitable treatment guidance, help arrange hospital communication, and provide follow-up support when requested.

No Website Storage

The consultation forms on this static website open a message draft and do not store submitted medical details in a website database. Information may still be processed by the communication channel you choose, such as WhatsApp or email.

Medical Information

Please share medical reports only after you are comfortable with the purpose of the request. Medical information should be shared through a consent-based process and only with the people involved in reviewing or coordinating your care.

Third-Party Services

This website may link to or embed third-party services, including WhatsApp, email providers, and YouTube. Those services have their own privacy practices and may collect data according to their policies.

Cookies And Analytics

This website does not require visitors to create an account. If analytics or advertising tools are added later, this policy should be updated before launch to explain what is collected and why.

Data Sharing

We do not sell patient information. Information may be shared with hospitals, doctors, coordinators, or service providers only when needed to respond to your request, support treatment coordination, or comply with applicable requirements.

Security

Before launch, the website should use HTTPS and secure hosting settings. Medical documents should be handled through secure, consent-based communication rather than public website comments or unsecured forms.

Your Choices

You can ask us to correct, update, or stop using information you previously shared, subject to reasonable operational, medical coordination, and legal limitations.

Contact

For privacy questions, email support@medtreatindia.com.
