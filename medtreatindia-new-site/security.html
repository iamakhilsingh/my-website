Privacy Policy

Initial enquiry only

The website form is meant for first contact. Please do not enter medical reports, passport details, payment information or highly sensitive medical information into the public enquiry form.

Secure document sharing

Medical reports should be shared only after coordinator guidance, through a consent-based process and not through a public website field.

WhatsApp and email limits

WhatsApp and ordinary email are useful contact channels, but they are not presented as dedicated secure medical-record portals for sensitive documents.
